Rob Castro

Rob Castro is the COO and operating partner of Nashville Automation Company, a firm founded at Bitcoin Park that designs and deploys custom AI systems and automations for small and midsize businesses. He runs client strategy, sales, and the relationships that hold every engagement together, from the first introduction call through the final sprint demo. In 2018 he founded MonzTech, an IT staffing firm he still operates today. Matthias Debernardini is the founder, CEO, and CTO of Nashville Automation Company, the firm he started to design and deploy custom AI systems and automations for small and midsize businesses.
