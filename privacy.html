Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 7, 2026

Nashville Automation Company ("we," "us," or "our") operates nashvilleautomation.io and provides agstaff, an AI assistant ("chief of staff") that, with your permission, works across your Google account to triage email, manage your calendar and tasks, and process your documents. This policy explains what we access, how we use it, where it is stored, and the choices you have. We keep it plain — the same way we build.

Information We Collect

  • Contact details you give us. When you book a call or email us, we receive your name, email address, and anything you choose to share about your business and its processes.
  • Google account data you connect. When you authorize agstaff, we access only the Google services you approve:
    • Gmail. To read and triage your inbox, summarize messages, and draft replies. If you enable it, agstaff can also send email on your behalf. agstaff never deletes your email. You choose which of these actions are allowed and can turn any of them off.
    • Google Drive. To open the documents and invoices you ask agstaff to process.
    • Google Calendar. To view and manage events so agstaff can keep your schedule in order.
    • Google Tasks. To read and update your to-do lists.
  • Basic site analytics. Standard technical information such as your browser type, device, and pages viewed, used only to keep the site working.

How We Use It

  • To run the assistant features you enable — triage and summarize email, draft and (if permitted) send replies, process documents, schedule and update calendar events, and manage tasks.
  • To respond to your questions and provide support.
  • To operate, secure, and improve the service.

Where Your Data Is Stored

To give the assistant memory and context across your requests, content from your connected Google account — including email content, documents, calendar events, and tasks — may be stored in our AI memory system on infrastructure we control, protected by access controls and encryption. This content is processed by the AI model providers we use (for example, Anthropic) solely to generate the assistant's responses for you; they do not use it to train their models. We keep it only as long as needed to provide the service and delete it when you disconnect or on request.

Google User Data — Limited Use

Our use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. Specifically:

  • We use your Google data only to provide and improve the user-facing features you enable.
  • We do not sell your Google data.
  • We do not use it for advertising.
  • We do not use it to develop, improve, or train generalized or non-personalized AI/ML models.
  • We do not allow humans to read your Google data, except with your explicit consent, for security or to comply with the law, or when the data has been aggregated and anonymized.
  • We share it only with service providers (such as our hosting and AI model providers), strictly to operate these features on your behalf.

You can revoke agstaff's access at any time from your Google Account permissions page.

What We Don't Do

  • We do not sell or rent your personal information to anyone.
  • We do not use your information for advertising or profiling.
  • We do not delete your email.
  • We do not collect more than the features you enable require.

Data Retention

We keep your connected Google content and contact information only as long as needed to provide the service to you. When you disconnect Google access or ask us to delete your data, we remove it from our AI memory system and records.

Your Choices

  • Choose which Google services and actions agstaff may use, and turn any of them off at any time.
  • Disconnect Google access entirely from your Google Account permissions page.
  • Request a copy or deletion of your data by emailing us.

Contact
