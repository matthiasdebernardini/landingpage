Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 7, 2026

Nashville Automation Company ("we," "us," or "our") operates nashvilleautomation.io. This policy explains what information we collect when you interact with us, how we use it, and the choices you have. We keep this deliberately short and plain — the same way we build.

Information We Collect

  • Contact details you give us. When you book a call or email us, we receive your name, email address, and anything you choose to share about your business and its processes.
  • Scheduling data. Booking a call is handled through Google Calendar. To create, view, or update the specific event for your call, we access only the calendar data needed to schedule that appointment. We do not read your unrelated calendar events.
  • Basic site analytics. Standard technical information such as your browser type, device, and pages viewed, used only to keep the site working and understand general traffic.

How We Use It

  • To schedule, confirm, and follow up on the free call you requested.
  • To respond to your questions and discuss whether we can help with your work.
  • To operate, secure, and improve this website.

Google User Data

Our use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. We access Google Calendar solely to schedule the appointment you request. We do not sell this data, do not use it for advertising, and do not share it with third parties except as needed to provide the scheduling feature or as required by law. You can revoke our access at any time from your Google Account permissions page.

What We Don't Do

  • We do not sell or rent your personal information to anyone.
  • We do not use your information for advertising or profiling.
  • We do not collect more than we need to talk to you and schedule a call.

Sharing

We share information only with the service providers that make this site and our scheduling work (for example, our hosting provider and Google for calendar scheduling), and only to the extent they need it to perform that service. We may disclose information if required by law.

Data Retention

We keep contact and scheduling information for as long as needed to serve you and for our ordinary business records, then delete it. You can ask us to delete your information at any time.

Your Choices

You can request a copy of the information we hold about you, ask us to correct or delete it, or withdraw Google Calendar access at any time. Email us and we'll take care of it.

Contact

Questions about this policy or your data? Email matthias@nashvilleautomation.io.
